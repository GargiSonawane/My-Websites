About my art

I love to draw. When I draw, I am at peace. The way how the pen moves and draws and how color pencils color in shapes keeps me relaxed. I try to draw as much as I can. I usually draw people, But sometimes I draw other things. My drawing style is anime, a style usually used in animation. I draw in my sketch book, But sometimes I paint things. I use sharpies, color pencils, and mechanical pencils. To paint, I use pencils, acryilic paint, and brushes of different styles. Anni Albers once said, "Art is something that makes you breathe with a different kind of happiness".

My Inspiration

My Inspiration is comic books. Comic books give me ideas on drawing and help me come up with ideas for characters. It is also something I read when I'm tired of drawing. Comic books also give me ideas for storylines (Don't worry, I don't copy). They give me ideas for new things for my stories. Comic books are the perfect blend of art and literature. This why it's my inspiration.
